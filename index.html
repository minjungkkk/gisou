FAQ

What does Gisou mean?

Gisou means women’s hair in Persian. It is a reference to Negin Mirsalehi’s Persian background.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 3-7 business days depending on location.

It will be my first time trying Gisou. What product do you recommend?

If we could make one recommendation that works for all hair types, we suggest you try our iconic Honey Infused Hair Oil. Based on a homemade family recipe with honey from the Mirsalehi Bee Garden, the Honey Infused Hair Oil is the ultimate multipurpose oil for frizz prevention, hydration, nourishment and shine. We offer it in a 20ml and 50ml bottle. However, what products you use all depends on your own hair care needs. For further recommendations, you can reach out to info@gisou.com.

Do you offer all-natural products?

Although our products contain natural ingredients, they are not 100% natural or vegan. We never communicate or promote our products to be 100% natural or vegan. However, we do promise that our products are created with all-natural, bee-sourced ingredients sustainably derived from the Mirsalehi Bee Garden, which will benefit your hair. The decision to include certain ingredients in our formulas is based on our belief that the result and the function of a product are just as important as the ingredients.
